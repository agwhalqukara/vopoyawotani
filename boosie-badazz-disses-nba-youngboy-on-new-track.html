Boosie Badazz fired shots at NBA YoungBoy in his new track, “I Don’t Call Phones I Call Shots.”

On “I Hate YoungBoy,” NBA dissed Boosie when he rapped: “Feel like Boosie don’t even like me, bitch, don’t call my phone (F*ck you).”

On his new song, Boosie responds: “First and last time speaking on this,/ If you felt that way, why you ain’t call me on my phone number? Bitch, you know you wrong/ Clearin’ all these n*ggas songs/ Don’t go clear a n*ggas song, but fake it like you a n*gga dog/ That’s that fake sh*t/ Don’t speak on me no more lil’ n*gga, you know I don’t go for that/ Let’s keep it real dog, you was a ho for that.”

YoungBoy also dissed several other people on the track, incluing Lil Durk, India Royale, King Von and Gucci Mane.

YoungBoy fans are waiting to see whether he will release another track responding to Boosie’s cut.
