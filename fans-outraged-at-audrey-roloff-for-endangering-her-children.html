Fans Outraged At Audrey Roloff For Endangering Her Children

August 07, 2024
blog

Little People Big World spoilers tease that Audrey Roloff is getting slammed again by LPBW fans. The latest outrage by fans stems from Audrey sharing her latest culinary creation with her children.

Little People Big World Spoilers – Dangerous Concoction

In a video that Audrey shared on Instagram, she let her young children try the raw batter from her homemade strawberry ice cream. Audrey also shared her recipe and the ingredients and it included things like raw milk, cream, and eggs. Raw dairy can be contaminated with harmful germs that can make anyone very sick, let alone a child. People who get sick from it might have diarrhea, stomach cramping, and vomiting.

Little People Big World Updates – Fans React

LPBW fans were quick to comment when they saw the photos and the captions, “This is a recipe for food poisoning!,” and “She is going to poison her kids!,” and “Drinking raw milk makes me nervous.”

This is not the first time that the Roloff mom has been slammed by fans. Back in May Audrey posted a video of the children, Ember 5, Bode 3, and Radley 1, on a four-wheeler with dad, Jeremy. Audrey tried to pass off the moment as a positive one, “Yes, these days are often long, exhausting, repetitive, demanding, and chaotic… but also full of wonder, awe, joy, laughter, learning, gratitude, and the beauty of simplicity. We truly are cherishing these days of our kids being little…” But fans did notice some alarming safety hazards – all were not wearing seatbelts or helmets. Here is what the fans had to say, “Jeremy, why are you riding the kids on the four-wheeler? That’s highly irresponsible and dangerous!” and “Without shoes or helmets.”

The last time Jeremy got slammed by fans, he had left Radley to stand alone in the cab of an excavator. LPBW fans were quick to react to that moment as well and many found it to be terrifying, “Would you leave your toddler in a car with an open door while you move your other vehicle? Same thing” and “Honestly, this s**t is terrifying. This is precisely how heavy equipment accidents happen.” and “Personally, living in a farm town accidents happen in the flash of an eye – and especially with children it is absolutely devastating.”
