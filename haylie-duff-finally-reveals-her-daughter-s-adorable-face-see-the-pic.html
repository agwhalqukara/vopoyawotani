Haylie Duff Finally Reveals Her Daughter's Adorable Face: See the Pic

July 09, 2024
blog

Be still, our hearts! Haylie Duff shared an adorable photo of her nearly 4-month-old baby girl, Ryan Ava, via Instagram on Tuesday, Sept.1, and her gal’s tiny grin is heart-meltingly cute.

“Our sweet baby girl #RyanAvaErhard Rosenberg is 16 weeks old today,” the first-time mom captioned the photo, in which little Ryan smiles wide-eyed up at the camera.

Earlier in the day, Duff shared a shot of a mischevious-looking Ryan in a pink onesie with the caption, “Baby’s first vacation.”

Duff, 30, and her fiancé, Matt Rosenberg, welcomed their bundle of joy into the world on Monday, May 11. “Both mom and baby are doing great!” a rep for the Real Girl’s Kitchen host told Us Weekly at the time. “Haylie and Matt are thrilled to finally meet their baby girl and become a family of three.”

Though this is Duff’s first go as a mom, she has had some significant support from younger sister Hilary, who’s had three years of parenting son Luca, 3.

“So many people have asked me, ‘What kind of advice has Hilary given you along the way?’ And she really hasn’t,” Haylie told Yahoo Celebrity in a recent interview. “She’s just been there for me. She hasn’t tried to push her way on me. There have been times where I’ve been physically overwhelmed, and she’ll just come over and hold the baby for an hour. Just physically being there has been the best thing she’s done for me in this process.”

The sisters are currently teaming up to start a Sister of Motherhood campaign, which they hope will help moms — first time and otherwise — create a supportive community together.

“No one really understands what being a mom is like until they go through it themselves,” Hilary told Yahoo. “Even watching from the outside, Haylie was always very supportive… So it’s been a really cool time for us to be really good to one another.”
