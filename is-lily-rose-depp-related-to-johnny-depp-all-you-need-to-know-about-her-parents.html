Is Lily-Rose Depp Related To Johnny Depp? All You Need to Know About Her Parents

September 25, 2024
blog

With her growing fame, many people are beginning to get curious about Lily-Rose Depp’s family background, especially whether she is related to the famous Hollywood actor Johnny Depp (and how).

Lily-Rose Depp is the daughter of Johnny Depp. She is Johnny Depp’s first child with his former partner Vanessa Paradis. Hence, it’s safe to say that Lily-Rose Depp is definitely related to Johnny Depp.

Read on to get to know Lily-Rose Depp a little bit more. We’ll look at her early life, her career so far, and answer a few more FAQs about this budding model and actress.

Who is Lily-Rose Depp?

Lily-Rose Depp is a young and talented American-French actress and model. She gained popularity through her roles in movies like “Yoga Hosers,” in which she appeared alongside her dad, and “The King” (with Timothée Chalamet, who was once rumored to have dated her).  Besides, her modeling career has also taken off with her campaigns for Chanel and other famous fashion brands. 

Family Background of Lily-Rose Depp:

Lily-Rose Depp was born on May 27, 1999, in Neuilly-sur-Seine, France, to her parents Johnny Depp and Vanessa Paradis. Now, who doesn’t know her dad? Her father, Johnny Depp, is a well-known actor and musician, famous for his roles in movies like “Pirates of the Caribbean,” “Edward Scissorhands,” and “Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street.”

The famous actor was 36 years old when he had his first child, Lily-Rose. In an interview with Oprah Winfrey in 2004, Johnny shared just how much his partner Vanessa and their kids had changed his life in a wonderful way. 

On the other hand, her mother, Vanessa Paradis, is a French singer, actress, and model. She gained fame at a young age through her hit song “Joe Le Taxi” in 1987. Although she’s a celebrity in her own right (and having been Depp’s longtime partner), Vanessa has always kept a relatively low profile.

Johnny Depp and Vanessa Paradis started dating in 1998 and share two children together, Lily-Rose and her younger brother, Jack Depp (born in 2002). However, the couple separated in 2012 after being together for more than 14 years.

Lily-Rose Depp’s Career:

Lily-Rose Depp began her acting career in 2014 with a small role in the indie movie “Tusk” where she played the character Girl Clerk #2. Directed by Kevin Smith of “Mallrats” fame, “Tusk” also featured well-known Hollywood names in its cast: Justin Long, Haley Joel Osment (famous for his role in “The Sixth Sense”), and of course, Johnny Depp.

She then appeared in another Smith-directed movie titled “Yoga Hosers” in 2016, where she played the lead role alongside Harley Quinn Smith. She has also appeared in other movies like “Savage” and “The King,” where she played Catherine of Valois opposite Timothée Chalamet, who played Hal/King Henry V of England. Lily-Rose starred alongside Natalie Portman playing her younger sister in the movie “Planetarium.”

Apart from her acting career, Lily-Rose Depp has also made a name for herself in the fashion industry. She has modeled for various top brands, the most known of which is Chanel, for whom she’s been a brand ambassador since 2016. She’s made the cover of famous magazines like Vogue and Vanity Fair as well. Her effortless style and unique fashion sense have made her a fashion icon for many, especially Gen Z. 

Quick Lily-Rose Depp FAQ

Q. Who are Lily-Rose Depp’s parents?

A. Lily-Rose Depp’s parents are Johnny Depp and Vanessa Paradis.

Q. Is Lily-Rose Depp related to Johnny Depp?

A. Yes, Lily-Rose Depp is Johnny Depp’s daughter.

Q. What is Lily-Rose Depp’s career?

A. Lily-Rose Depp is an actress and model. She has appeared in movies like “The King” and “Yoga Hosers” and she is a brand ambassador of Chanel.

Lily-Rose Depp is a talented young actress and model who has made a name for herself in the entertainment industry. Although she may be considered a “nepo baby” thanks to having famous parents, her unique fashion sense and impressive acting skills prove that she truly fits her current stature as a budding icon in the entertainment industry.
