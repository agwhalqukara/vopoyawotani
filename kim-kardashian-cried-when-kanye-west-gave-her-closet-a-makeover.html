Kim Kardashian Cried When Kanye West Gave Her Closet a Makeover

August 29, 2024
blog

If Kim Kardashian’s Cinderella, then Kanye West was her clothing fairy godmother back when they started dating in 2012. The “Only One” rapper, 37, gave the reality star, 34, a closet makeover at the start of their relationship, which was documented on an episode of Keeping Up With the Kardashians. 

Years later Kardashian landed the April 2014 cover of Vogue with her hubby and has attended countless fashion events (including the Met Gala), often applauded for her bold style choices. In a recent interview on Live With Kelly and Michael, North West’s mom recalled the moment West cleaned out her closet. 

“He had his stylists come in and they put everything that he thought wasn’t cool enough in a pile, and I walked in and it was a pile to the ceiling of shoes — all my amazing shoes that I loved,” she said. “And I started crying. I was like, ‘I can’t get rid of this stuff, your stylist has no idea what she’s talking about.’ So I put it all in another room, and I was like, ‘I’ll trust your opinion, but I’m not getting rid of my stuff.’” 

But West didn’t just take, he also gave.
